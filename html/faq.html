FAQ, Need Help?

Find quick answers to the most common questions about ordering, tailors, and payments.

What if the size is wrong?

Yes, you can request free adjustments within a limited period depending on the tailor.

How do I know the tailor is trustworthy?

Each tailor has reviews, ratings, and sample work images.

Is shipping available?

Yes, shipping is available locally or nationwide. The cost appears before payment.

What payment methods can I use?

Online payment or Cash on Delivery if supported by the tailor.

Can I modify the design after payment?

Sometimes yes, depending on the production stage.

What fabrics are available?

You choose the fabric from the tailor's available options with photos and pricing.

What happens if the tailor cancels my order?

You receive a full refund according to the payment method's timeline.
